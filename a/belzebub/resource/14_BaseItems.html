BASE ITEM TYPES

This list enumerates all equippable item types in Diablo 1 HD. It consists of armor and weapon types of Normal (white) quality. It can be useful in choosing the one that fits your needs, considering their Requirements, Armor Class and/or Attack Speed. Lastly you must hover the pointer over the dotted text to see the Attack Speed, since each character class has a different one.

Note! All these items can spawn in game as drops or at NPC vendors, also of Magic quality, and if you gambled for them they can spawn as Rare quality and many of them even as Set or Unique quality items. But be aware that some affixes will enhance the stats and also required level. Nevertheless these affixes do not carry over when you craft an item, and only the base stats are taken into consideration and the required level will increase according to the new affixes that have appeared after crafting.
