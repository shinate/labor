DIFFICULTY LEVELS

As you might know the original Diablo game had 3 difficulty levels, well Diablo 1 HD mod now features a 4th difficulty. Upon defeating Diablo our character can remove the Soulstone from prince Albrecht, Diablo's innocent host, and thus unlock the next difficulty level.

Normal
Character Level progression : 1-27

The first and easiest difficulty level.


Nightmare
Character Level progression : 27-35

A significant step up in difficulty. There is a 25% resistances penalty for your character.


Hell
Character Level progression : 35-42

Difficulty is further increased. There is a 50% resistances penalty for your character.


Torment
Character Level progression : 42-??

Abandon all hope ye who enter. There's a 75% resistances penalty for your character.



PLAYER X

It's a cool feature that may not be known by all players and can be used in SinglePlayer games if your character is powerful enough. It makes all battling monsters tougher while also increases their rewards, to correspond with a MultiPlayer game with X players in it.


During a single player game press Enter and type the following:

$player X

(where X is a number from 1 to 10) Press Enter again and the game will confirm your action.

- monster health points, armor class, hit chance and damage will be boosted
- experience gained from killing monsters is increased
- monsters are more likely to drop more items of higher item level
