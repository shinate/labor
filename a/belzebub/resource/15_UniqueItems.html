UNIQUE ITEMS

This list enumerates all the unique items found in Diablo 1 HD. Since all Armor pieces and Weapon types have 2 tiers you can uptier the Tier 1 ones if you find and learn the Upgrade Unique Armor/Weapon recipe, but this boosts only their base stats (Armor Class, Damage, Durability and sometimes Requirements).

Note! All these items can spawn in game as drops or by gambling at NPC vendors.
